📚 Rules of Life

🟢 Birth

A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive.

💀 Death (Lonely)

A live cell with fewer than 2 neighbors dies of isolation.

💀 Death (Crowded)

A live cell with more than 3 neighbors dies of overcrowding.

✨ Survival

A live cell with 2 or 3 neighbors survives to the next generation.
